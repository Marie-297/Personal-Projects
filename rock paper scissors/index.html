RULES IN PLAYING

The rock is a closed fist; paper is a flat hand with fingers and thumb open and extended; and scissors is a fist with the index and middle fingers fully extended toward the opposing player.

Rock wins against scissors.

Paper wins against Rock.

And scissors wins against paper.

Same choice result in a Tie.
